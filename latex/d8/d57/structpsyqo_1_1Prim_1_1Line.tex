\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1Line}{Line} primitive. 

The \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1Line}{Line} primitive will draw a single, flat-\/colored segment. It will be drawn between the {\ttfamily pointA} and {\ttfamily pointB} vertices.
