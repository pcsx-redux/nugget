\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1Rectangle}{Rectangle} primitive. 

This primitive will draw a rectangle of arbitrary size. The {\ttfamily position} member specifies the location within the screen where the top left corner of the rectangle will be rendered. The {\ttfamily size} member specifies the size of the rectangle. The color is specified by the constructor parameter, or the {\ttfamily set\+Color} method.
