\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1FlushCache}{Flush\+Cache} primitive. 

This primitive will flush the \doxylink{classpsyqo_1_1GPU}{GPU}\textquotesingle{}s cache. It\textquotesingle{}s meant to be used after changing a texture or a CLUT, as the \doxylink{classpsyqo_1_1GPU}{GPU} has a small texture cache. The primary usage should be when doing render-\/to-\/texture. The {\ttfamily \doxylink{classpsyqo_1_1GPU}{GPU}} class will use it for its {\ttfamily upload\+To\+VRAM} method.
