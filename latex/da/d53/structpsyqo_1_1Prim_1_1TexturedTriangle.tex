\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A textured triangle. 

This primitive will draw a textured triangle. It will be drawn between the {\ttfamily pointA}, {\ttfamily pointB}, and {\ttfamily pointC} vertices. The primitive has weird-\/looking ordering of members, but it is necessary to accommodate the way the hardware wants the triangle information to be sent to it. The attributes of the primitive can be better visualized with this order\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily pointA}, {\ttfamily pointB}, {\ttfamily pointC}
\item {\ttfamily uvA}, {\ttfamily uvB}, {\ttfamily uvC}
\item {\ttfamily clut\+Index}, {\ttfamily tpage} 
\end{DoxyItemize}
