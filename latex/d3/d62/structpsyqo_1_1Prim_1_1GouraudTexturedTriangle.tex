\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A textured, blended triangle. 

This primitive will draw a textured triangle with its texels blended with the interpolated color values of its vertices. It will be draw between the {\ttfamily pointA}, {\ttfamily pointB}, and {\ttfamily pointC} vertices. The primitive has weird-\/looking ordering of members, but it is necessary to accommodate the way the hardware wants the triangle information to be sent to it. The attributes of the primitive can be better visualized with this order\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily pointA}, {\ttfamily pointB}, {\ttfamily pointC}
\item {\ttfamily colorA}, {\ttfamily colorB}, {\ttfamily colorC}
\item {\ttfamily uvA}, {\ttfamily uvB}, {\ttfamily uvC}
\item {\ttfamily clut\+Index}, {\ttfamily tpage} 
\end{DoxyItemize}
