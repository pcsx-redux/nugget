Whether to insert nops after register operations. 

The \doxylink{namespacepsyqo_1_1GTE}{GTE} is a coprocessor, and as such, register operations are not guaranteed to be completed before the next instruction. This means that triggering a \doxylink{namespacepsyqo_1_1GTE}{GTE} operations right after writing to a register may result in the operation using the old value of the register. To avoid this, nops can be inserted after register operations to ensure that the register is updated before the next \doxylink{namespacepsyqo_1_1GTE}{GTE} operation. Use the {\ttfamily Safe} option to insert nops, or the {\ttfamily Unsafe} option to avoid them, if you are sure that the register will not be used immediately after the operation. \begin{DoxyEnumFields}{Enumerator}
