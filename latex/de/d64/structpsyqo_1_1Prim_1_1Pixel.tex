\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1Pixel}{Pixel} primitive. 

This primitive will draw a single pixel. The {\ttfamily position} member specifies the location within the screen where the pixel will be drawn. The color is specified by the constructor parameter, or the {\ttfamily set\+Color} method.
