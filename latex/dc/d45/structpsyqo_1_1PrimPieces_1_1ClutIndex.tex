\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A primitive\textquotesingle{}s CLUT command. 

This is a common building block piece used in primitives. The binary representation is meant to be the same as the CLUT argument for \doxylink{classpsyqo_1_1GPU}{GPU} commands. The constructor can take either a {\ttfamily \doxylink{unionpsyqo_1_1Vertex}{Vertex}} or the raw x and y coordinates for the CLUT to use.

Remember that CLUTs are aligned to 16 pixels, so the vertex coordinates are rounded to the lowest multiple of 16 on the X axis.
