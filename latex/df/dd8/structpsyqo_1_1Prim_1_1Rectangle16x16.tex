\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The 16x16 \doxylink{structpsyqo_1_1Prim_1_1Rectangle}{Rectangle} primitive. 

This primitive will draw a rectangle of 16 by 16 pixels. The {\ttfamily position} member specifies the location within the screen where the top left corner of the rectangle will be rendered. The color is specified by the constructor parameter, or the {\ttfamily set\+Color} method.
